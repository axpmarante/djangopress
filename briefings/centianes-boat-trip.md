# Centianes Boat Trip — Site Briefing

## Business
Centianes Boat Trip is a boat tour operator based at Praia de Vale Centianes in Carvoeiro, Lagoa — the heart of Portugal's Algarve coast. They specialize in guided excursions to the world-famous Benagil sea caves and the stunning coastline between Vale Centianes and Praia da Marinha, visiting around 20 caves per trip. Operating year-round from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, they offer a range of tours from standard group departures to private charters, all aboard the "CENTIANES" — an 8.6-meter vessel powered by a Suzuki 325hp engine.

The company has built an outstanding reputation, ranked #1 in Nature & Parks in Lagoa on TripAdvisor with a perfect 5.0/5 rating across 566+ reviews. Guests consistently praise the skilled crew — guides José and Louis and Captain Johnny — for their professionalism, humor, and deep knowledge of the coastal formations. Reviewers highlight the company's safety-first approach: they conduct sea and weather checks before every departure and offer full refunds when conditions aren't suitable, earning trust as a "very safety conscious and honest" operation.

Their target audience is international tourists visiting the Algarve — couples, families, and small groups looking for an authentic, thrilling, yet safe way to explore the region's iconic marine caves and hidden beaches. The tone should be adventurous yet trustworthy, capturing the excitement of speeding through crystal-clear waters into dramatic cave formations while reassuring visitors of safety and professionalism. Tours are affordably priced at €25 for adults and €12.50 for children (3-10), with free transfers from Carvoeiro included.

What sets Centianes Boat Trip apart is their departure point: Vale Centianes beach offers the closest access to the Benagil caves, meaning shorter travel time and more time exploring. The smaller boat allows access to caves that larger vessels cannot enter. Combined with competitive pricing, personalized attention, and overwhelmingly positive reviews, they are the go-to choice for cave exploration in the Carvoeiro area.

- Mood: Fresh and adventurous meets modern and clean — the site should evoke the thrill of ocean exploration while feeling polished and trustworthy. Think crisp ocean blues, lots of whitespace, high-quality imagery of caves and coastline.
