# Windmill Restaurant — Site Briefing

## Business

Windmill Restaurant is an exclusive fine dining experience housed in a beautifully restored 1938 windmill in Albufeira, Algarve, Portugal. With only 12 seats available per evening, this adults-only establishment offers one of the most intimate and romantic dining experiences in the region. The restaurant is run by António and Patricia, a young couple passionate about hospitality and Mediterranean-inspired Portuguese cuisine.

The dining room sits at the top of the historic windmill, with thick original walls preserving the building's character and east-facing windows offering stunning views over Albufeira and the sea. The restaurant operates exclusively by reservation, with two seatings per evening — an early seating around 6:30-7:00pm and a later seating around 9:00-9:30pm, with an outside terrace option at 7:30-8:00pm during warmer months. A deposit is required for parties of 4 or more guests.

The menu is a seasonal 3-course tasting experience priced at approximately €70 per person, featuring carefully selected starters, mains, and desserts that highlight quality Portuguese produce with Mediterranean inspiration. Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options are available upon request when booking. The restaurant also offers private dinners for exclusive events and gift vouchers for special occasions.

Windmill Restaurant has earned an exceptional reputation in the Algarve, with a 4.8-star rating on TripAdvisor (496+ reviews), 4.7/5 on Restaurant Guru (1,064+ reviews), and an 8.3/10 on Hotspots Algarve. Guests consistently praise the impeccable service, beautifully presented dishes, knowledgeable and welcoming hosts, and the uniquely romantic atmosphere of dining inside a historic windmill. The tone is sophisticated yet warm — an intimate evening rather than a formal affair.

Note: Access to the restaurant involves a steep staircase, which may not be suitable for guests with mobility challenges. The restaurant is closed on Sundays and Mondays, operating dinner hours from 6:00 PM to 10:30 PM.

## Design Preferences

- Color palette: Dark, sophisticated tones — deep charcoal (#2C2C2C), warm gold/champagne accents (#C9A96E), off-white for text (#F5F0EB), subtle dark backgrounds. Think candlelit elegance.
- Typography: Elegant serif font for headings (Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond), clean sans-serif for body text (Lato or Inter). Generous letter spacing on headings for a refined feel.
- Mood: Luxurious, romantic, intimate, exclusive. The design should evoke the feeling of a special evening out — warm candlelight, fine wine, personal attention. Not stuffy or pretentious, but genuinely warm and sophisticated.
- Spacing: Generous whitespace between sections. Let the content breathe. Each section should feel like its own moment.
- Imagery: Rich, warm-toned photography. Evening/dusk lighting. Close-ups of plated dishes, the windmill silhouette, candlelit tables, sea views.
