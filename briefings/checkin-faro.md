# Check In Faro by Leonel Pereira

## Business

Check In Faro is a contemporary Portuguese fine dining restaurant by Michelin-starred Chef Leonel Pereira, located in Faro's historic centre. The restaurant occupies a beautifully restored former cereal and wine storehouse on Rua do Castelo, blending heritage architecture with modern gastronomy.

Chef Leonel Pereira is an Algarve native and one of Portugal's most acclaimed chefs — named Chef of the Year 2015 and winner of the 2019 Platinum Fork award from the prestigious "Boa Cama Boa Mesa" guide. The restaurant holds the Michelin Bib Gourmand distinction for excellent quality-to-price ratio.

The dining concept is built around a "boarding pass" journey through the chef's career. The menu is divided into evocative sections — Check-in, Cozinhados no Tacho, Momentos Especiais, and Check-out — inviting guests to travel through time while discovering the best of Portuguese ingredients. Every dish emphasizes sustainability, seasonality, and deep respect for local produce.

The intimate 26-seat dining room and 30-seat terrace create an atmosphere that is refined yet relaxed — the chef describes it as "trendy, modern, laid-back." Dinner service only, Monday through Saturday.

The tone should be sophisticated but warm and approachable — reflecting the chef's philosophy of "honest food full of flavour to share."

## Design Preferences

- Colors: Dark, sophisticated palette — deep charcoal or dark navy as primary, warm gold or champagne as accent, cream or off-white for text on dark backgrounds
- Fonts: Elegant serif for headings (like Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond), clean sans-serif for body text
- Mood: Refined, intimate, warm — like the restaurant itself. Think candlelit dinner, not sterile fine dining
- Reference: The existing site at checkinfaro.pt has a clean, modern feel
