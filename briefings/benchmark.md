# Benchmark Cafe — Site Briefing

## Business
Benchmark Cafe is a modern coffee shop and co-working space in downtown Lisbon, Portugal. Opened in 2022, it combines specialty coffee with a productive work environment. The space seats 40 people across a cafe area and a quiet work zone with fast Wi-Fi, power outlets at every table, and meeting rooms available by the hour.

The target audience is remote workers, freelancers, and digital nomads who want great coffee and a reliable place to work. The tone should be friendly, modern, and no-nonsense.
